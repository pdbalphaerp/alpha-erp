Appointment Calender

This module integrates Odoo appointments with Google Calendar so you can easily attach a Google Meet videoconference link to any meeting you schedule for your clients. When creating or confirming an appointment, users can generate or select the corresponding Google Calendar event and automatically retrieve its Meet link, then share it with the customer via email, SMS, portal or any communication channel used in Odoo. The link is stored on the appointment record, making it simple for staff to find and resend, and ensuring both parties join the same virtual room at the right time. By centralizing video meeting details inside Odoo, this module streamlines remote appointments, reduces confusion about which link to use, and delivers a more professional online meeting experience.

Key Features

  • Google Calendar video link selection

    Choose or generate a Google Meet videoconference link directly from the appointment in Odoo, without manually copying URLs from Google Calendar.

  • Link stored on the appointment record

    Save the Google Meet URL on each appointment so staff can easily find, open or resend the same video link at any time.

  • Automatic inclusion in reminders

    Insert the video link into email or SMS reminders and confirmations sent from Odoo, so clients always receive the correct meeting URL.

  • Support for multiple Google calendars

    Work with different Google Calendar accounts or calendars and attach the appropriate Meet link depending on the user or team handling the appointment.

  • Timezone aware scheduling

    Keep appointment times consistent between Odoo and Google Calendar, reducing confusion about meeting time when working across timezones.

  • Improved remote appointment experience

    Centralize video meeting details in Odoo so both staff and clients always use the same, correct Google Meet link, reducing mistakes and last‑minute confusion.
